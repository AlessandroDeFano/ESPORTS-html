Welcome

We are Esports.com

This is an international championship in which all esports teams compete to determine the winner who will receive our cup. We organize tournaments of all games, from League of Legends, Valorant and Rocket League to Call of Duty, FIFA and Fortnite and many others.
The Esports.com platform was founded in 2022 by four competitive gaming enthusiasts.
Our goal is to push esports and gaming into the mainstream. To reach that goal we work on a lot of different formats and present our motivation on a range of different platforms such as TV and social media.
On our website you can find guides, news, interviews and exclusive content to the most popular games in the esports, gaming and streaming industry. You can find our social links at the bottom of our website.
